# Product Context: Curious Magpie Website

## Why This Site Exists

Marjolein IJbema runs a ceramics atelier called Curious Magpie. She makes handmade outdoor ceramics (garden decorations, mushrooms, snails, vegetable markers, etc.) and sells them at local markets in the Groningen/Drenthe region. This website serves as her online presence to showcase products and share market dates.

## Problems It Solves

1. **Visibility**: Gives Curious Magpie an online presence beyond Instagram
2. **Market Discovery**: Visitors can find upcoming market dates and locations
3. **Product Showcase**: Shows the range of handmade ceramics available
4. **Contact**: Easy way for potential customers to reach Marjolein

## Key User Experience Goals

- **Warm & Playful**: Design reflects the handmade, nature-inspired character of the products
- **Fast Loading**: No build step, static files, instant page loads
- **Easy to Update**: Marjolein (or an AI assistant) can update market dates and products by editing HTML
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Many visitors will browse on their phone

## What the Site Provides

1. **Home page**: Hero section, about the atelier, featured products, CTA to agenda
2. **Products page**: 6 product cards with images and descriptions
3. **Agenda page**: Upcoming market dates with locations and times, past markets
4. **Contact page**: Email, Instagram, and info about visiting markets

## Content Language

All content is in **Dutch** (Nederlands).
